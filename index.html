ABOUT ME


Professional Motion Graphics Designer, 3D artist, and illustrator / PS compositor

I'm drawn to well thought out and polished pieces, I believe that mastery can be accomplished only by working hard at ones craft. At the same time I admire the simplicity, cleverness and elegance of good minimalism which is not easy to achieve. My inspiration comes from the joy of creating something that will make me feel proud of my work, I love challenging myself to create beautiful pieces. As a motion graphic designer, illustrator and 3D artist I aim to use my sense of aesthetic and experience to bring creative projects to life.
